About Us

Your Gateway to Professional Computer Education.

Micronext Computer Education is a premier computer training institute with 3 branches across Vasai-Virar region. Established with a vision to provide quality computer education to students, professionals, and businesses, we offer comprehensive courses from basic computer literacy to advanced programming and web design. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum, we have trained thousands of students who are now successfully placed in IT companies, MNCs, and running their own businesses.

Our Vision

To become the leading computer education provider in Vasai-Virar region by delivering quality education with practical training that makes our students industry-ready.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with IT skills that enhance their career prospects and enable them to thrive in the digital economy.
